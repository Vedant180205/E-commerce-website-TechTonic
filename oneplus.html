OnePlus Nord CE 4

The OnePlus Nord CE 4 offers smooth performance with the Snapdragon 7 Gen 3, 120Hz AMOLED display, super-fast 100W charging and OxygenOS refinement.   • Camera: 50MP OIS main + 8MP ultrawide

  • RAM: 8GB
  • Storage: 128GB / 256GB
  • Battery: 5500mAh
  • Charging: 100W SUPERVOOC
  • OS: OxygenOS based on Android 14
  • 5G Support: Yes
