Asus Vivobook S 15

Rs 1,04,990/-

The Asus Vivobook S 15 OLED is a sleek and lightweight laptop powered by Qualcomm's AI-enhanced processor, delivering efficient multitasking and intelligent performance. It features a stunning 15.6-inch OLED display with vibrant colors and deep blacks, ideal for creators and entertainment. With AI-powered noise cancellation and long battery life, it ensures seamless productivity and immersive experiences. Packed in a stylish, durable design, it balances cutting-edge technology with portability.

Features:

  • Processor: Qualcomm Snapdragon 8cx Gen 3 AI-Enhanced
  • Display: 15.6-inch OLED, 2.8K resolution, 120Hz refresh rate
  • Graphics: Integrated Qualcomm Adreno GPU
  • Memory: Up to 16GB LPDDR4X RAM
  • Storage: Up to 1TB PCIe NVMe SSD
  • Operating System: Windows 11
  • Audio: AI-powered noise cancellation, Harman Kardon-certified speakers
  • Connectivity: Wi-Fi 6E, Bluetooth 5.2
  • Ports: USB-C, USB-A, HDMI, 3.5mm audio jack
  • Battery: 67Wh battery, up to 10 hours of usage
  • Build: Lightweight and durable design, aluminum chassis
  • Weight: Approx. 1.8kg
